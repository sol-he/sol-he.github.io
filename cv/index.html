I dreamt of becoming a concert pianist and somehow wandered into math. I studied music at Brandeis University for a year before transferring to Carnegie Mellon University as a math major. In my undergrad, I did some programming on the side in order to get a job after graduation. Fortunately, the day before Thanksgiving in my senior year, a professor encouraged me to realize that I still prefer math over other career choices. Now I'm a graduate student at University of Nebraska-Lincoln.
