About

I adopted my name "Sol" when my high school Spanish teacher asked us to pick a Spanish name for the class. Sol means the sun in Spanish, and I love sunshine and nature. The video on the home page was taken in the Hulunbuir Grassland. I aspire to herd sheep and horses there.

In my free time, I like to walk in neighborhoods and look at houses. My other hobbies include playing frisbee, singing, salsa dancing, playing the recorder, and laying around.
